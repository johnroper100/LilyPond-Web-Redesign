Beautiful Sheet Music

LilyPond is a music engraving program, devoted to producing the highest-quality sheet music possible. It brings the aesthetics of traditionally engraved music to computer printouts. LilyPond is free software and part of the GNU Project.

Powerful and Flexible for Every Musician

Classical Engraving

LilyPond allows musicians to produce elegant sheet music that is easy to read.

Text Input

LilyPond processes text input, which contains all of the content of your score.

Extensible Design

All layout settings can be changed to suit your personal typographical taste.
